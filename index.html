ABOUT US

Our bakers start work before the sun rises each day to ensure that every batch is as fresh as possible before it gets to you. We're dedicated to kickstarting your morning with the best quality Asian (Hong Kong Style) buns, pastries, cakes, hot cooked food & breakfast, our authentic Hong Kong Style Milktea and more! Fun fact: We've been in the bakery business for many years, and have gained excellent feedback since!

Handcrafted Natural Goods

We use natural ingredients for our pastries and buns. Absolutely no preservatives and coloring in our dough.

Our Philosophy

Healthy and great tasting food is the way of enjoying life!
